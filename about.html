Meet Our Founder

Asoluka Charles

Asoluka Charles founded the HAVE IT ALIVE FOUNDATION from a deeply personal place. Having grown up in an underserved community, he witnessed firsthand how easily potential can be extinguished by a lack of resources and opportunity. His life's mission became to create a support system that doesn't just provide aid, but actively works to keep the "it"—the hope, the dreams, the spark of life—alive in every individual we help.

With a background in community development and a heart for service, Alex channeled his experiences into a foundation built on empathy, resilience, and direct action.

Our Journey and Its Challenges

The road to making a difference is never easy. In our early days, our passion often outpaced our resources. We faced significant challenges that tested our resolve but ultimately strengthened our mission.

  • Resource Scarcity: We started with little more than a vision, struggling to secure funding, find volunteers, and gather necessary supplies.
  • Building Trust: Gaining the trust of communities that have been let down time and again was a slow, delicate process that required consistency and genuine care.
  • Logistical Hurdles: Reaching remote and isolated individuals meant overcoming complex logistical nightmares, from transportation to communication barriers.

These struggles taught us to be more innovative, resourceful, and deeply grateful for every bit of help we receive. They are a core part of our story and a reminder of why this work is so vital.

A Heartfelt Thank You

None of our work would be possible without you. To every person who has donated, volunteered their time, shared our story, or simply offered a word of encouragement—we thank you from the bottom of our hearts.

Your support is the fuel that allows us to overcome challenges and continue our mission. You are the reason we can keep hope alive for so many. You are a part of this foundation, and together, we are making a tangible difference in the world.
