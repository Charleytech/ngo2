Our Purpose & Strategic Focus

The core purpose of the HAVE IT ALIVE FOUNDATION is to combat poverty and foster self-reliance in underserved communities. We target key areas that create a ripple effect of positive change: food security, education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. Our goal is not just to provide aid, but to build lasting capacity.

Key Programs for the Year

Green Shoots Initiative

Providing seeds, tools, and modern farming training to rural farmers to boost food security and create sustainable livelihoods.

Women's Enterprise Hub

Empowering women with vocational skills like tailoring, soap making, and financial literacy to start their own small businesses.

Sponsor-A-Child Education

Ensuring children from low-income families stay in school by covering tuition, uniforms, and learning materials.

Community Health Outreach

Organizing free medical check-ups, health seminars, and providing basic medical supplies in remote areas.
